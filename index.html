David Klein

Post Doc at the Max Planck Institute for Security and Privacy.

I’m a postdoc at the Max Planck Institute for Security and Privacy in Bochum in the System Security group of Thorsten Holz. My research focus is in the area of Web Security, Application Security and Privacy. On the public Web, I work on discovering the prevalence of various vulnerabilities and privacy violations, mostly leveraging dynamic taint tracking.
